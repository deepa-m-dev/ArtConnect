About ArtConnect

ArtConnect is a digital space where creativity meets technology. It is designed to help artists showcase their artwork online and share their visual stories with a wider audience beyond physical boundaries.

Many talented artists struggle to find platforms where their work can be presented professionally and discovered easily. ArtConnect was created to bridge that gap by providing a simple, elegant, and accessible online gallery experience for both creators and viewers.

Artists can upload and manage their artwork collections, while visitors can explore diverse creative expressions through an immersive gallery interface built to highlight artistic individuality.

Built using modern web technologies including Spring Boot, MongoDB, Thymeleaf, and Cloudinary, ArtConnect combines cloud storage with full-stack development to create a responsive and scalable art-sharing platform.

ArtConnect is more than a gallery; it is a step toward building a connected digital community for emerging artists.
